Scalable, Adaptive, and Resilient Autonomy (SARA)

Xiaoyi (Jeremy) Cai, Lakshay Sharma, Michael Everett, Jimmy Queeney

About

ACL is part of the MIT team that participates in the ARL SARA program (cycle 2). The goal of the project is to achieve autonomous navigation in complex off-road environments. To this end, the ACL team has proposed several approaches for uncertainty-aware traversability learning and risk-aware planning:

  • Physics-Informed Evidential Traversability Learning
  • EVORA: Deep EVidential Traversability Learning for Risk-Aware Off-Road Autonomy
  • RAMP: A Risk-Aware Mapping and Planning Pipeline for Fast Off-Road Ground Robot Navigation
  • Risk-Aware Off-Road Navigation via a Learned Speed Distribution Map
